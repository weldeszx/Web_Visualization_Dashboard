Summary: Latitude and Longitude

The purpose of this analysis was to identify trends in weather patterns as they relate to latitude. It was completed by randomly generating 1500 possible values for both latitude and longitude. The Python library citypy was used to generate city names closest to the randomized latitude and longitude. Data was retrieved from the OpenWeatherMapAPI forecast data. It included 5 days worth of forecast data given in 3 hours intervals; 40 records per city data point. Each point of interest, temperature, humidity, cloud cover, and wind speeed, was then averaged and plotted versus latitude for analysis.

The graph shows the location of the cities analyzed. The color of each point represents temperature with green and red representing the lowest and highest average temperature respectively.
